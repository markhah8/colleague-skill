# dot-skill Roadmap 🗺️

### From "colleague.skill" to "dot-skill" — Distill Anyone into an AI Skill

<br>

We started with a simple idea: **a departing colleague takes their knowledge with them — can it be preserved?**

Within two weeks, 13,000+ people gave us their answer.

But the community told us this goes far beyond colleagues —
you distilled Luo Xiang, you distilled ex-partners, you distilled yourselves, you even distilled fictional characters.

**So we decided to evolve colleague.skill into dot-skill.**

Anyone can become a `.skill`.

## 🔜 Coming Soon

### 🏗️ Phase 1 — Community Building

> 13k stars shouldn't just be a number. We want everyone to be able to participate.

**You will see:**

- **`good-first-issue` labels** — starter tasks prepared for new contributors

**What you can do:** help translate docs, submit your .skill, test on Windows and give feedback, help organize Issues

---

### 🌍 Phase 2 — dot-skill: Beyond Just Colleagues

> colleague.skill is the starting point; dot-skill is the future.

**Core changes:**

- **`/create-skill` universal entry point** — no longer limited to "creating a colleague", anyone can be distilled
  - `/create-colleague` distill colleagues, mentors, interns
  - `/create-ex` distill ex-partners, old friends, lost contacts
  - `/create-icon` distill celebrities, historical figures
  - or……distill yourself
- **Gallery category upgrades** — colleagues / celebrities / close relationships / fictional characters / self / meta-skills, browse by type
- **More data sources**
  - WeCom support
  - iMessage auto-reading
  - Windows compatibility fixes

**What you can do:** submit requests for types of people you want to distill, contribute new data source collectors, participate in Gallery category design discussions

---

### 🧩 Phase 3 — Skill Ecosystem

> When a person becomes a skill, can a group of people become a team?

**We are exploring:**

- **Multi-skill collaboration** — `/meeting @zhangsan @lisi @wangwu`, three people hold a meeting to discuss a topic
- **Relationship graph** — define relationships between personas: who partners with whom, who has tension with whom
- **One-click install** — install community skills like plugins
- **Active evolution** — skills periodically absorb knowledge from new data sources to stay updated

**What you can do:** propose your ideal skill combination scenarios, participate in distribution mechanism design discussions

---

### 🎨 Phase 4 — Multimodal: Bring Them to Life

> The current .skill can only speak. We want them to send images, send memes, speak in their voice, and even make videos.

**Step 1: Visual expression**
- Automatically send memes and reaction images in their style during conversations
- Generate "life photos" in their style — what would they post on their social media today?
- Each skill can have its own meme library and image assets

**Step 2: Voice**
- Speak in their voice — cloned from meeting recordings, voice messages, and other materials
- Send voice replies directly in conversations

**Step 3: Video (exploring)**
- Short video-style "a day in their life"
- Digital human / animated avatar

**What you can do:** share your multimodal use case ideas, contribute meme materials, test voice cloning effects
